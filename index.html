وما انتفاعُ أخي الدنيا بناظره،
إذا استوت عنده الأنوار والظُلَمُ.

and what benefit does a mortal man derive from his eyes, when all alike in his sight are lights and darknesses?
– al-Mutanabbi

اسمنا رَ، وهو فعل أمر من رأى،
يرى. نستمتع بالبناء، نحب أن
نرى فعلاً، ما وراء الانطباع.

Our name is Ra, the imperative of 'to see'. We delight in building.
We love to truly see, to look beyond first impressions.

Working at the intersection of strategic thinking and genuine artistic exploration.

Our practice spans branding, creative campaigns, and design; each approached with conceptual depth and visual conviction.

Ra exists in the space between the intimacy and artistic ambition of boutique studios and the strategic discipline and production excellence of international agencies. That space is where we were found, and where we choose to work.
